here we will explain how they workand how good these new items are. this will be balanced based on most items in the game.

Robotic weapon

The weapon has 45 bullets and at a medium firing rate. it deals 25 damage per hit and a headshot does 55 damage. great for destroying large groups in a short amount of time.

Smart spear

A spear that tracks enemies and explodes on contact. the spear can travel at a fast speed, but only lasts 10 seconds before it falls. it deals 75 damage and can be good in case of a tight situation.

Tech suit

A slightly taller exoskeleton with a cannon and two arms which can be used to gather more materials faster and easier than by hand. it has 500 health and can be used in a combat state, but that drains the battery. the battery is a large core inside the back which allows it to move.
